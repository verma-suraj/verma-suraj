About

I'm an Electronics & Communication Engineering student with a deep passion for building things that bridge hardware and software.

My work spans the full embedded development lifecycle — designing schematics in KiCad, routing PCBs with impedance and EMI analysis, writing firmware in C/C++, integrating sensors and actuators, and deploying IoT systems with cloud telemetry.

I'm currently a Research & Product Development Intern at Ninetynine Labs, working on EV charging infrastructure, smart energy systems, and embedded technologies. Alongside that, I'm part of Team Nabhayan, engineering an autonomous drone for ISRO-URSC 2026.

I'm driven by the challenge of taking a concept and building the complete system required to make it a reality.
